About GIS Radiology

Delivering fellowship-trained expertise, timely reads, and patient-centered care nationwide.

Who We Are

Global Imaging Specialists (GIS) was founded in 2011 by a like-minded group of physicians aiming to provide the highest caliber of professional radiology services and patient care. We are a team of fellowship-trained and board-certified radiologists and cardiologists, providing subspecialty diagnostic reads and interventional services.

Experience You Can Trust

Our physicians have extensive experience across hospital and outpatient settings and deliver accurate, effective reads in a timely manner. A multi-specialty approach and prompt communication with referring physicians drive the best patient outcomes.

  • Clear, clinically relevant reporting
  • Direct consult lines & call-backs
  • Structured QA & peer review

Coverage & Partnership

Given the versatility and size of our group, we offer around-the-clock coverage. As an integral part of the clinical team, we support clinical education and community awareness—helping partners grow service lines and referral bases for long-term stability and impact.

  • 24/7/365 availability
  • Scalable daytime, after-hours, and overflow support
  • Growth-minded partnership with measurable results
